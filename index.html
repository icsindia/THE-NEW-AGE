About Us

Maruti has redesigned the Baleno’s front end, making it more aggressive thanks to a new front bumper, a 3D design for the grille and new fog lamp housings. The 16-inch alloy wheels now get a new dual-tone design as well. Inside, there’s a new 7.0-inch SmartPlay infotainment system that debuted in the new Wagon R, and a dual-tone black-and-blue interior color scheme.

OUR BALENO

Prices for the Baleno facelift range from Rs 5.45 lakh to Rs 8.77 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). This marks a price hike over the older model, ranging from Rs 7,000 to Rs 21,000 for the petrol and diesel manual variants, and between Rs 30,000 to Rs 44,000 for the CVT variants.
